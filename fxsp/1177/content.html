About The Teams...

Jacksonville
Jaguars

The Jags resisted the temptation to take the splashy Johnny Football and took the solid Blake Bortles instead. Then then got two steals in Marquise Lee and Allen Robinson in Round 2.

Cleveland
Browns

Johnny Football is now Johnny Cleveland. But the Browns also got a freak athlete in CB Justin Gilbert and a road paver in Joel Bitonio. And don’t sleep on Terrance West or Pierre Desir.

Tampa Bay
Buccaneers

The Bucs boosted their offense by taking a great pass catching threesome in Vincent Jackson, Austin Seferian-Jenkins and Mike Evans. RB Charles Sims is a good addition to the fireworks.

Minnesota
Vikings

Toddy Bridgewater might be the most underrated player in the Draft and will immediately compete for the starting job. Anthony Barr is a perfect pass rusher in Mike Zimmer’s system.

Houston
Texans

If you like athleticism, Jadeveon Clowney is your guy. If he plays hard, he could be one of the greats. Xavier Su’o-Filo is a mauler and QB Tom Savage is a QB that has a lot of potential.
