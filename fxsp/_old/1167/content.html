About The Teams...

Arizona

The early season favorites have had some bad luck lately, but the ‘Cats are still a team that could get hot at the right time.

Winning Ingredients:

Nick Johnson, Brandon Ashley and Aaron Gordon.

Duke

Mike Krzyzewski is doing another masterful job as the Blue Devils ACC trials will have them tournament ready.

Winning Ingredients:

Jabari Parker, Quinn Cook, Rodney Hood and amile Jefferson.

Florida

The SEC might be down, but don’t discount the Gators’ demolition of the conference, including a big win over Kentucky.

Winning Ingredients:

Casey Prather, Scottie Wilbekin and Michael Frazier III.

Syracuse

No longer undefeated, Coach Jim Boeheim’s Orange is still playing some of their best basketball in years.

Winning Ingredients:

C.J. Fair, Trevor Cooney, Jerami Grant and Tyler Ennis.

Wichita State

Coach Gregg Marshall is taking the Shockers to new heights, who have a great chance at going undefeated.

Winning Ingredients:

Fred VanVleet, Tekele Cotton, Cleanthony Early and Ron Baker.
