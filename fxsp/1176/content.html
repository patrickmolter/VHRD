About The Players...

Derek Carr
QB - Fresno State

Carr might have the strongest arm of any QB in the Draft. His excellent Pro Day turned a lot of heads, since he was in the hospital the night before

Teddy Bridgewater
QB - Louisville

The 6-foot-3, 195-pound quarterback has an excellent career at Louisville playing for task-master Charlie Strong. His duel-threat skills could have a huge impact early.

Johnny Manziel
QB - Texas A&M

After being the first freshman to win the Heisman Trophy, Manziel followed it up by another stellar campaign. His free-lancing ability makes him a big play waiting to happen.

Blake Bortles
QB - UCF

Looking for the prototypical quarterback, who fits the mold of other notable Pro Bowlers? Look no further than Bortles. The 6-foot-5, 232lbs QB has the size to endure NFL-sized hits.

Zach Mettenberger
QB - LSU

As a big, strong-armed prospect and a prototypical dropback passer, Mettenberger is a throwback. While his lack mobility is a negative, he can make any throw.
