About The Teams...

Arizona
Cardinals

Strong down the stretch last season, the Cards actually played the 49ers and the Seahawks tough. ‘Zone will lean on a stout defense to make the playoffs.

Baltimore
Ravens

The Ravens followed up a Super Bowl win with a forgettable 8-8 in 2013. But with John Harbaugh at the helm and Joe Flacco under center, a turnaround is likely.

Indianapolis
Colts

Andrew Luck has led the Colts to 11-5 in each of his first two seasons. If Indy can find a more consistent running game, they might be an AFC Force.

Philadelphia
Eagles

The Nick Foles/LeSean McCoy might be the best QB/RB combo in the NFL. In Year Two, Chip Kelly’s offense might be the most potent in the league.

San Diego
Chargers

2013 was one of Phillip Rivers best, leading SD to the playoffs where they crushed Cincy. Another year under coach Mike McCoy could mean big things.
