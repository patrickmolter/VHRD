About The Teams...

Jadeveon Clowney
DE - South Carolina

Physically, Clowney is an athletic freak. A 6-foot-5, 266-pound monster who ran a 4.47 in the 40-yard dash at the Combine would give any NFL team a weapon on defense.

Sammy Watkins
WR - Clemson

Watkins could be a game changer. He displayed his exceptional speed in Indianapolis, where the 6-foot-1, 211-pounder ran a sub-4.4. in the 40-yard dash.

Johnny Manziel
QB - Texas A&M

After being the first freshman to win the Heisman Trophy, Manziel followed it up by another stellar campaign. His free-lancing ability makes him a big play waiting to happen.

Blake Bortles
QB - UCF

Looking for the prototypical quarterback, who fits the mold of other notable Pro Bowlers? Look no further than Bortles. The 6-foot-5, 232lbs QB has the size to endure NFL-sized hits.

Khalil Mack
OLB - Buffalo

Some draft analysts have tabbed Mack as the most gifted player in the draft. Weaker college competition aside, Mack is one of the best edge players to hit the draft in years
